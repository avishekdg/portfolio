Unlocking Revenue Growth: A Data-Driven Approach for Airbnb Hosts in Dallas
This project leveraged feature engineering, data analysis and machine learning to predict Airbnb host revenue in Dallas for the next quarter. By employing advanced ML techniques, including Gradient Boosting and Random Forest, we achieved a remarkable predictive accuracy of around 99.35%. However, for sake of interpretability, we chose Backward Regression as our final model with predictive accuracy of 71%. Our findings offer actionable insights for hosts to make informed investment decisions, dynamically adjust pricing, and enhance overall business strategies, ultimately unlocking revenue growth in the competitive hospitality marketplace.
Python, ML, Optimization
